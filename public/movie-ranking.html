Rajamouli's all time Hits

1. Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017)

Verdict:
Amarendra Baahubali, the heir apparent to the throne of Mahishmati, finds his life and relationships endangered as his adoptive brother Bhallaladeva conspires to claim the throne.

2. Baahubali: The Beginning (2015)

Verdict:
A child from the Mahishmati kingdom is raised by tribal people and one day learns about his royal heritage, his father's bravery in battle and a mission to overthrow the incumbent ruler.

3. RRR (2022)

Verdict:
A fictitious story about two legendary revolutionaries and their journey away from home before they started fighting for their country in the 1920s.

4. Eega (2012)

Verdict:
A murdered man is reincarnated as a housefly and seeks to avenge his death.

5. Magadheera (2009)

Verdict:
A warrior gets reincarnated 400 years later, after trying to save the princess and the kingdom, who also dies along with him. He then sets back again to fight against all odds and win back his love.

6. Vikramarkudu (2006)

Verdict:
A thief's life changes after he discovers a little girl who claims he is her father, and what follows is violence after a gang hunting for his lookalike closes in on him.

7. Chatrapathi (2005)

Verdict:
Displaced Sri Lankans in a Vizag port are ruled by a local rowdy. This is the story of Chatrapati Sivaji who overcomes this oppression and how he reunites with his long lost mother and brother.

8. Simhadri (2003)

Verdict:
Simhadri, a loyal servant in a landlord's house, also leads a secret life as Singamalai, a powerful leader in the state of Kerala.

9. Maryada Ramanna (2010)

Verdict:
A man returns to his parents' village to claim his land. Along the way, he befriends a charming young woman. Unbeknownst to him, her family has a long-standing feud with his family.

10. Thief of Yama (2007)

Verdict:
A lovable scoundrel's life is cut short when he angers the god of death; however, he still has a few tricks up his sleeve.

11. Challenge (2004)

Verdict:
Students of a college form a Rugby team and play against a violent gang of thugs to get back their college land.

12. Student No. 1 (2001)

Verdict:
Aditya joins a law college as per his father's wishes though he wanted to be an engineer. He faces many problems while trying to reform the unruly students along with dealing with his terrifying past.
